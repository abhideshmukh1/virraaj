Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour

Est. 2024

Born from Passion, Rooted in Tradition

Virraaj is more than a restaurant — it is a love letter to the subcontinent's extraordinary culinary tapestry. Our chefs travel the length and breadth of India sourcing heirloom grains, hand-ground spice blends, and age-old recipes passed down through generations.

Every plate is a canvas. We reimagine classical Indian cooking through the lens of modern gastronomy — preserving the soul of each dish while elevating it with contemporary technique, seasonal produce, and breathtaking presentation.

Step inside and let your senses wander. From the lingering perfume of slow-braised lamb to the delicate sweetness of saffron-kissed desserts, a meal at Virraaj is an immersive journey you will carry with you long after the last course.

The Culinary Visionary

Chef Arjun Mehta

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Chef Arjun Mehta brings over two decades of culinary mastery to Virraaj. Trained at the Culinary Institute of America, he honed his craft in the kitchens of three-Michelin-starred restaurants across Paris, London, and New York before returning to India with a singular mission — to redefine Indian fine dining on the world stage.

His philosophy is simple yet profound: respect the ingredient, honour the tradition, and dare to innovate. Named one of Food & Wine's "Best New Chefs" and featured in The World's 50 Best Restaurants spotlight, Chef Arjun's cuisine is poetry on a plate.

  • 🏆 Best Chef — National Restaurant Awards 2025
  • ⭐ Featured in Asia's 50 Best Restaurants 2025
  • 📕 Author of Masala Moderne (2024)
