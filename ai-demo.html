Marketing Orchestration

Automated Guest Journey

From first enquiry to loyal advocate — every touchpoint is intelligently orchestrated.

  1. 🔍

    Discovery & Acquisition

    AI analyses search intent, social engagement, and lookalike audiences to run hyper-targeted Instagram, Google, and Zomato campaigns — attracting the right guests at the right moment.

    ⚡ Trigger: New audience segment identified
  2. 📧

    Pre-Visit Personalisation

    48 hours before each reservation, AI sends a bespoke welcome email with personalised menu highlights, drink pairings, and the evening's chef specials — all tailored to the guest's taste profile.

    ⚡ Trigger: Reservation confirmed + 48h before visit
  3. 🍽️

    In-Experience Intelligence

    Real-time table analytics alert front-of-house staff to milestone moments (anniversaries, birthdays), dietary preferences, and service timing — enabling proactive and genuinely personal hospitality.

    ⚡ Trigger: Guest seated + occasion flags detected
  4. ⭐

    Post-Visit Engagement

    2 hours after dining, guests receive an AI-crafted thank-you message with a personalised review invitation, recipe card, or cocktail guide. NPS score is captured and routed for action.

    ⚡ Trigger: Bill settled + 2 hours elapsed
  5. 🔄

    Loyalty & Re-Engagement

    AI monitors visit cadence and sends tailored win-back campaigns when a loyal guest hasn't visited in 45+ days — offering exclusive chef's table access, seasonal menu previews, or curated gift experiences.

    ⚡ Trigger: 45+ days since last visit for loyal guest
